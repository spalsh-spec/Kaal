## Slide 2 — The Problem

**Visual:** Split screen. Left: Developer with 10 tabs, API docs, terminal. Right: Marketing director with a confused expression.

**Headline:** 97% of the workforce is being left behind.

**3 points:**

**Speaker note:** "We tested this. We gave a random selection of professionals $500 in AI credits and told them to go faster. 12 weeks later, 80% had given up. Not because they weren't smart — because nobody built the bridge."
